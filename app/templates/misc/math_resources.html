Mathematics Research Resources

01
ArXiv.org - Mathematics Section

ArXiv is a preprint repository where researchers share their latest work before formal publication. The Mathematics Section of ArXiv encompasses a wide range of mathematical disciplines, providing an open-access platform for the dissemination of cutting-edge research and ideas.

Visit Site
02
MathSciNet

MathSciNet is a comprehensive database covering the world's mathematical literature. Developed by the American Mathematical Society (AMS), MathSciNet is an essential tool for researchers, providing access to reviews, abstracts, and bibliographic information for a vast array of mathematical publications.

Visit Site
03
Zentralblatt MATH

Zentralblatt MATH is an international database covering pure and applied mathematics. It serves as a valuable resource for researchers, offering reviews, abstracts, and bibliographic information on a wide range of mathematical topics, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange in the global mathematical community.

Visit Site
04
Project Euclid

Project Euclid provides high-quality mathematics and statistics scholarship. With a focus on open access, Project Euclid offers a platform for hosting and accessing peer-reviewed journals, books, and conference proceedings, contributing to the dissemination of rigorous mathematical research.

Visit Site
05
JSTOR - Mathematics Journals

JSTOR offers a curated collection of academic journals, books, and primary source materials in the field of mathematics. Researchers and students can access a wealth of scholarly content, including historical archives, to support their studies and exploration of mathematical topics.

Visit Site
06
American Mathematical Society (AMS)

The AMS website serves as a hub for information on publications, conferences, and resources for mathematicians. As a leading professional organization, AMS plays a crucial role in advancing mathematical research and education, connecting mathematicians worldwide, and promoting the development of the discipline.

Visit Site
07
Institute of Mathematical Statistics (IMS)

IMS is an organization dedicated to the development and dissemination of the theory and applications of statistics and probability. The IMS website provides a gateway to statistical research, conferences, and publications, fostering collaboration and advancements in statistical science.

Visit Site
08
Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM)

SIAM focuses on applied and computational mathematics, offering a wealth of resources including journals, conferences, and other materials. It serves as a platform for the exchange of ideas and research in the intersection of mathematics and various applied fields, addressing real-world challenges.

Visit Site
09
Google Scholar - Mathematics

Google Scholar is a freely accessible web search engine that indexes the full text or metadata of scholarly literature across a wide array of publishing formats and disciplines. The Mathematics section of Google Scholar provides a convenient tool for researchers to discover relevant mathematical publications and academic resources.

Visit Site
10
Mathematics Genealogy Project

The Mathematics Genealogy Project provides information about the academic lineage of mathematicians. By tracing the advisor-advisee relationships, it offers insights into the historical development of mathematical research and the connections between generations of mathematicians, fostering a sense of community and tradition in the discipline.
