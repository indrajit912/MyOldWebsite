ISI PhD Registration Form
This LaTeX template is designed for the PhD registration form of the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore Center. To get started, download the .zip file, extract it on your local computer, and locate the 'main.tex' file within. Modify the initial \FirstName, \MidName, etc., within this file.
